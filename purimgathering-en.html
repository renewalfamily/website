A Family That Cannot Be Shaken

In today’s great shaking, though darkness covers the earth and thick darkness is over the peoples, we must pay attention to the light of God's glory that is about to be released through His people (Isaiah 60:1-4)! In 2022 the Holy Spirit revealed Isaiah 22:22 as the key verse of the year. In 2012, the western Church has handed over the key of authority to the Chinese church. Now is the time for the Chinese. God will raise up the authority of the church to overcome the gates of hell (Matthew 16:18)! Open the door to the nations for the Lion of Judah, for the King of glory will come in (Psalm 24:7)!

The Lord leads us to visit the ancient paths and walk in them (Jer. 6:16). On the first Purim, Esther and Mordecai walked together as two generations, leading the whole family of Israel in one accord, to destroy the power of Haman and save the whole nation of Israel! This year Purim happens to fall on the same day as fire festival in Iran. It is the moment when the two kingdoms collide again. In the midst of the great shaking of the world, the Lord instructed us to gather His Chinese bride together on Purim, to demonstrate a Kingdom that is completely different from the world, a Kingdom that cannot be shaken! We have to show that there is an "unshakable family" in this unshakable kingdom! This Kingdom family is the most powerful weapon of war as well as harvest in the last days, to attract all people to the Lord Jesus (John 12:32, John 13:35)! In this unshakable family of covenant and love, we will turn defeat into victory, and turn sorrow into joy, like the first Purim!

Although the world is full of bad news, we witness and report the good news in our Father’s House! Because of the love of this family, Holy Spirit is awakening the Chinese identity of the Chinese younger generation overseas; because of the intercession of this family, three spiritual mothers experienced significant healing in body soul and spirit! Heavenly Father also gave us a special surprise: Messianic father Pastor Asher Intrater and Arabic mother Rania Sayegh will be joining together to bless the Chinese family as we enter the destiny of bringing the Gospel back to Jerusalem!
