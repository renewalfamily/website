Pastor Andrew & Norine's testimony

Awaken the dawn Aug 1, 2022: Pastor Andrew & Norine Brunson's testimony

After serving in Turkey for 23 years doing churchplanting, training, helping refugees, and building a house of prayer, they were falsely accused of terrorism in October 2016. Andrew was detained in Turkish prisons for two years. He was dramatically released in October 2018 due to the prayers of Christians around the world and political pressure from the U.S. government.

Pastor Andrew and his wife love the Lord deeply and their powerful testimony shows us what the most important preparation for the end-time bride is. They are still focused on the Muslim world, the persecuted church, and preparing Christians for end-time persecution.
