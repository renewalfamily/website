5779 Feast of Trumpets(2)

Sep 9, 2018

2018-09-09

The Lord says unto you My dear beloved spiritual soldiers. In such a holy celebration I have to release transparency of spirituality and harmonization.

The Lord says, I will let churches of Vancouver to be a model for the churches of the nation and the churches of all nations. And this is a very powerful mobilization to prepare My remnant in the whole world to be ready enough for the preliminary signs of the great tribulation. Such preliminary signs of the great tribulation are now happening - war, killings, persecutions. So, be ready to be victorious。

And the Lord says, as long as you are willing even to be martyred I am in the business of preparing the precious inheritance in heaven. The Lord says unto you. There is no time to waste. You have to be willing to stand steadfastly and no room for sluggishness, says the Lord. Amen.

5779 Feast of Trumpets(1)

Sep 7, 2018

The Lord says unto you, My Dear Beloved Followers whether pastors or deacons, indeed it’s a historical moment in which I am Gathering you together to envision what I am about to release in the near future and in the meantime, I will bring about My divine schedules.

And for your surprise, I will astonish you with My divine schedule. I will show up the instrumentality of togetherness that will bring about My Presence wherever you go.

The Lord says unto you, I am training you to be spiritual fighters to stand in the gap on behalf of the whole city in Vancouver not only for Vancouver, but for the universal church.

The Lord says unto you, such Gatherings will be happening every now and then. And for your surprise, I will attract many ones outside the local church. The Lord says you will zealously taste such togetherness.

And the Lord says I will gift you, many of you, My supernatural gifts. And the Lord says even in your businesses I will reveal My wisdom.

The Lord says in this meeting, you will experience something beyond measure to the point that you will come back home with a determination to be fully devoted. The Lord says the cloud of My glory is over you now. Says the Lord, amen.
